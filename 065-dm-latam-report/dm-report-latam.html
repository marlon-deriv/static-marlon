Moving forward

Challenges

  • We faced posting delays in Q1/2023, mainly due to Design work overload. (9/12 posts published each month on average)
  • There were a few “publishing errors” on falcon.io due to platform issues. We are addressing it with Falcon CS to reach a final solution.

Achievements

  • We reached the 1K followers milestone on our Spanish Instagram channel entirely organically.
  • Our followers doubled, and engagement increased on our Portuguese Instagram channel due to our PT Conference coverage in Paraguay.
  • 2 videos were produced and released with decent engagement before our professional equipment acquisition.

Goals

  • We plan to engage in and cover LATAM Affiliate Marketing seminars, conferences, and trips.
  • We aim to increase affiliate presence on our social media.
  • We aim to produce good quality, educational reels/videos with people presence, at least 1 every 2 months.
  • We plan to introduce regional industry news on our Facebook & Instagram stories, at least twice a month.
