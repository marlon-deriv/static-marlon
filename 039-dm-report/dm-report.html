Challenges

Social media content

We have been unable to publish separate content for FB, IG & Twitter due to limited resources. We're working on simplifying and streamlining the content production process in the upcoming year to offer more variety on different SM platforms.

Video content

We want to publish more video content on social media as videos are most engaging. Due to the long video production timelines, we are unable to do so. We're working on reels for Instagram and client educational videos for YouTube to increase our video content.

EU emails

Frequent content changes due to regulatory requirements have hindered the production of marketing email campaigns to EU. We are working closely with content, design and compliance teams to streamline the process and produce emails directed to the EU markets at a faster rate.
